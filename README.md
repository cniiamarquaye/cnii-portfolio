## What I Do

I translate complex spatial data into decision-ready insights.

My work focuses on building and applying spatial models that explain environmental processes, quantify risk, and support planning across water systems, land systems, and climate-related challenges.

---

## What I Bring

I don’t just run analyses — I structure problems and deliver interpretable outcomes.

1. **Spatial Modelling for Real Systems**
I develop models that represent how environmental systems behave across space and time, from surface processes to subsurface dynamics.

2. **Data → Insight → Decision Support**
I connect geospatial data, modelling outputs, and domain knowledge to produce results that can actually be used in policy, planning, or operations.

3. **Integrated Geospatial Workflows**
I combine GIS, remote sensing, and numerical modelling into reproducible pipelines that move beyond isolated analysis.

4. **Interpretation of Complex Outputs**
I focus on what results mean — not just what they show — translating technical outputs into clear, defensible conclusions.
